Description of files in "Data/Other" folder

* Note: All files are used in the "travel_behavior.R" script in the "Analysis" folder. 

* AQ_2019_Cache.csv
  - Daily air quality data for Cache County in 2019. 

* mybg_new.xlsx, block_groups.xlsx
  - Block groups selected for recruitment. 
* tl_2019_49_bg.shp, tl_2019_16_bg.shp
  - Shapefiles of 2019 block groups for Utah and Idaho. 

* cache_weather.csv, cache_weather.rds
  - Daily weather data for Cache County in 2019.
